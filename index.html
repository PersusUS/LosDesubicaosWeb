Proyect Details

Educational Component of the Game

The game’s planet discovery system, inspired by real techniques used by space agencies, simplifies complex methodologies. This allows players of all skill levels to understand and engage with the content, offering an accessible astrophysicist experience.

Simulated Astronomical Observation

Players can use an in-game telescope to view a simulated celestial sphere with moving celestial bodies. By zooming in, they can interact with these objects through various mechanics, such as observing a planet transit in front of its star, measuring brightness changes, and capturing images in different light wavelengths. This realistic experience enhances players' understanding of astronomical exploration and the observational techniques used by astrophysicists.

Game Narrative and Educational Goals

In The Event Horizon, you play as an aging researcher residing in a lighthouse on an asteroid, tasked with exploring space. The character faces the dual challenges of a missing daughter and amnesia resulting from an unknown trauma, complicating his quest to find her.

Gameplay and Story Progression

As you progress in the game, you must explore the lighthouse's library to find files on nearby exoplanets to remember your daughter's name. You'll rediscover these planets using real detection techniques akin to those developed by NASA. In the final stages, as you uncover these celestial objects, the mystery of your daughter's fate unfolds, triggering a surge of memories, including her name and her groundbreaking research on black holes. The game skillfully combines a compelling narrative with educational elements, providing an immersive experience that captivates players while teaching real-world scientific principles.

Use of AI

The team consists of seven members, including Perseo, an AI developed by Jesús Pérez Bazarot with capabilities similar to ChatGPT-4. Perseo has acted as an additional team member, refining multilingual English texts and ensuring their sophistication. It also has a programming module that has accelerated web development and a ComfyUI module for generating visual content like images and logos. Overall, Perseo has been a key collaborator, boosting the team's efficiency and helping manage the project's complexity.
